👉 “I don’t understand DP”

# 🧭 How to Follow This Roadmap

## 🔁 For EACH problem:

1. Try **recursion first**
2. Convert → **Memoization**
3. Convert → **Tabulation**
4. Optimize space (if possible)

---

# 🧠 DP Thinking Template

For every problem, ask:

* What is my **state**?
* What is my **choice**?
* What is the **transition**?
* What is the **base case**?
